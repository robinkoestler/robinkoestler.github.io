Book Corner

A collection of interesting books I read in the form of their notable quotes.

It is not important how many books you read, but which ones - this is a simplification of a Seneca quote:
"You must linger among a limited number of master-thinkers, and digest their works, if you would derive ideas which shall win firm hold in your mind."
— Seneca, Letters to Lucilius (Letter 2)

Disclaimer: I am neither a professional book reviewer, nor a professional writer. All of the content is strictly personal, full of opinions, which you or anybody else might not share. If you like to comment on them, feel free to reach out to me. Needless to say, every review contains spoilers right off the bat.
